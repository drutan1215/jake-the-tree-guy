About Jake

18+ Years
In The Trees

We specialize in hazardous and hard to reach tree pruning and removals. I climb every tree personally and have over 18 years experience in all aspects of the industry.

We won't bring any big bucket trucks in your yards and I'm constantly being told we do work that other companies had to turn down. Exceptional care is given when doing final cleanup — I guarantee it will be just as clean if not cleaner than when we arrived!

No job is too big or too small. Fully insured and always free estimates. I promise you will be smiling when we leave! I follow ISA (International Society of Arboriculture) guidelines for Pruning and work is done in accordance with the (ANSI) A300 Standards.

Our Services

Tree Pruning & Trimming

Expert pruning following ISA guidelines and ANSI A300 standards to promote health, safety, and the natural beauty of your trees.

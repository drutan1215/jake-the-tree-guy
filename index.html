About Jake

18+ Years
In The Trees

"We're often called for the jobs other companies turn down — and we take pride in completing them safely and professionally."

We specialize in hazardous and hard-to-reach tree pruning and removals, with over 18 years of hands-on experience in the tree care industry. Every tree is climbed personally, allowing us to handle difficult jobs with precision, control, and care.

Unlike companies that rely on large bucket trucks, we avoid bringing heavy equipment into your yard whenever possible. From start to finish, your property is treated with respect. Final cleanup is a priority — we guarantee your yard will be left just as clean, if not cleaner, than when we arrived.

No job is too big or too small. We are fully insured, offer free estimates, and follow ISA pruning guidelines with work performed in accordance with ANSI A300 Standards. Our goal is simple: safe, reliable tree care and a customer who is smiling when we leave.

Our
Services

From hazardous removals to precision pruning — we handle every job with expert care. Click any service to learn more.

01 Hazardous Tree Removal 🌲 +
Specializing in dangerous, dead, and storm-damaged trees that other companies turn down. Jake personally climbs every tree and safely removes what others can't reach or won't touch.
02 Tree Pruning & Trimming ✂️ +
Expert pruning following ISA guidelines and performed in accordance with ANSI A300 standards to promote tree health, improve structure, and enhance the natural beauty of your trees.
03 Hard-to-Reach Removals 🧗 +
Tight spaces, steep slopes, trees close to structures — we access what others can't. Jake climbs every tree personally, with the right equipment for the job and exceptional care for your property.
04 Stump Grinding 🪵 +
Complete stump removal ground below surface level, leaving your yard clean and ready for whatever comes next. No ugly stumps, no trip hazards, no regrowth.
05 Emergency Services ⚡ +
Storm damage, fallen trees, structural hazards — we respond fast. When a tree is threatening your home or family, you need someone who can get up in it and deal with it safely. That's us.
06 Free Estimates 📋 +
Every job starts with a free, no-pressure on-site estimate. Honest pricing, no hidden fees, no upsells. Call or fill out the contact form and we'll come take a look at no charge.
07 Animal Rescue 🐾 +
Got a cat, raccoon, or other animal stuck up in a tree? Jake will climb up and bring them down safely — no charge, ever. Animal rescues are always free. If someone or something needs help getting out of a tree, we're there.
08 Drone Rescue 🚁 +
Drone tangled up in the branches? We can get it down without damaging your equipment or the tree. With 18+ years of precision climbing experience, recovering a snagged drone is all in a day's work.

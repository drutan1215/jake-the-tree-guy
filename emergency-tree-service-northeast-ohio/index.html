Northeast Ohio storms don't schedule themselves. A line of thunderstorms, a blast of ice, an unexpected windstorm — and suddenly you have a tree resting on your roof, blocking your driveway, or leaning dangerously close to your family's bedroom window.

Jake The Tree Guy LLC handles emergency tree situations throughout Northeast Ohio. Based in Stow, we respond across Summit, Portage, Stark, Cuyahoga, and surrounding counties. Jake personally assesses and removes every hazardous tree — the same experienced hands every time, fully insured.
